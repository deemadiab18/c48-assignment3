INDICATOR PROFILE

Case definitionPatients attending the Emergency Department from registration time until first physician assessment.
Quality Measure NameEmergency Department Waiting Time
Departments includeEmergency Department
RationaleMeasures service efficiency and speed of care; improves patient satisfaction and reduces risks from delayed assessment/treatment.
Type of measureProcess Measure
NumeratorTotal waiting time (minutes) from registration to first medical assessment during the measurement period.
Data sourceED Information System or registration records.
Included populationAll patients registered in the Emergency Department during the measurement period.
Excluded populationPatients who left before being seen; records with missing registration or assessment times.
Denominator statementTotal number of patients medically assessed during the measurement period.
FormulaAverage Waiting Time = Total Waiting Time (minutes) / Number of Patients Evaluated
Target≤ 30 minutes (or according to hospital policy)
Data reported asAverage (minutes)
Frequency of measurementMonthly
Sample techniqueConsecutive Sampling (all cases during the period)
Sample sizeAll eligible patients
Data collected byNursing Supervisor / Quality Officer
Data analyzed byQuality and Performance Improvement Department
Data reported toQuality Committee, Hospital Administration, Emergency Department Head
